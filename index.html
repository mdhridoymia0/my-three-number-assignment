Program Sections

A professional hockey club typically consists of several program sections, each with a specific function and responsibility.

Junior Program

The program offers coaching, training, and mentoring from experienced and qualified staff, as well as exposure to competitive and challenging environments.

Teenager Program

Teenager Program is a program that aims to provide young hockey players with the opportunity to develop their skills and pursue their passion for the sport.

Professional Program

Professional Program is a new initiative that aims to provide high-quality training, coaching, and mentoring for aspiring hockey players who want to pursue a career in the sport.
